1. What is DART?

This "Descriptive Analysis & Revision Test" series is framed by an innovative team of Doctors/ selected students of government medical colleges/ experienced NEET experts to optimise your performance, accuracy & consistency which will open the door to your dream medical college.

2. Why DART is different from other test series ?

DART is strictly based on current syllabus of NTA (NEET ) Each and every question is framed by our experienced team to make this test series reliable, authentic & utilitarian . Test paper followed by complete Video solutions by our mentors. DART provides PTRS ( Post Test Response System) after every single test to improve the system for the betterment of our aspirants . After test DART provides the results to all our participants in the form of Students Assessment Report (SAR) in pdf format.
